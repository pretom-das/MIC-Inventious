UI/UX DESIGN COMPETITION

Calling all creative minds! This competition is your chance to showcase your user interface (UI) and user experience (UX) design skills.

Register Now

Target Audience

This competition is open to all aspiring UI/UX designers

Competition Format

  • Individual Competition: Open to all aspiring UI/UX designers.
  • Pre-Registration: Participants must submit a home page design for any mobile or web application during registration. Submissions will be reviewed to shortlist participants for the final presentation.
  • Device Requirements: Participants must bring their own devices for the final presentation.
  • Final Presentation:Shortlisted participants will present their projects physically on the final day. Judges will evaluate designs based on creativity, usability, innovation, and functionality.

Judging Criteria

Judges will evaluate teams based on creativity, design ability .

Prizes

  • Champion Team: 10,000 BDT
  • Runner-Up Team: 6,000 BDT
