Event Details

Inventious 4.1 bridges the advancement of the Fourth Industrial Revolution with the spirit of innovation. With the tagline 'Renovate Towards Revolution,' this national festival calls on students from across the country to showcase their creativity, leadership, and problem-solving skills. Spanning three segments like Lead the Future, UI/UX Design Competition and Project Showcasing, bringing together students, leaders, and innovators for a unique experience of learning, competition, and networking
