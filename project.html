PROJECT SHOWCASING

Do you have a groundbreaking project that deserves recognition? Project Showcasing at Inventious 4.1 is your chance to impress a distinguished panel of experts and fellow innovators!

Register Now

Target Audience

This segment is open to innovators and tech enthusiasts with exciting projects to showcase.

Competition Format

  • Team Formation: Teams can have up to 3 members.
  • Pre-Registration: Submit your project's title, abstract, and a graphical abstract during the initial registration phase. Shortlisted teams will be notified via email and invited to register for the final round.
  • Final Presentation:Selected teams will present their projects physically on the final day of the event. Power connections will be provided, but participants are responsible for bringing any other necessary equipment.

Judging Criteria

The organizing committee reserves the right to make final decisions in case of unforeseen circumstances.

Prizes

  • Champion Team: 10,000 BDT
  • Runner-Up Team: 6,000 BDT
